ABOUT KHEL HINDUSTAN

Khel Hindustan is a web application that automates the process of applying for the approval of sports infrastructure projects under the Khelo India Scheme.

In any given year, the Ministry receives a large number of such proposals in accordance with the forms outlined in the Scheme's Operational Guidelines.These proposals are submitted to a comprehensive screening and evaluation process before being approved in compliance with the Scheme's guidelines. The entire procedure is not only time-consuming and complex, but also demands a massive amount of paperwork and manual processing.

The fundamental purpose of Khel Hindustan is to alleviate the vast workload and laborious processing involved in the application process by digitalising it, in which we collect data from applicants completely via web questionnaires.The system also aids in the review and evaluation of the proposal, lowering the time necessary to sanction a project proposal.The technology also assists in the proposal's review and evaluation, reducing the time required to sanction a project proposal.
